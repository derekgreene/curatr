Curatr — Sign In

Curatr is an online platform which provides access to the British Library Digital Collection, corresponding to over thirty-five thousand unique titles, both fiction and non-fiction, from 1700 to 1899. The platform is specifically designed for humanities scholars and offers comprehensive search functionality by keyword, author, category, and date.

Curatr — Sign In

Curatr is a bespoke online platform designed to improve the accessibility of the British Library Nineteenth Century Digitised Books Collection (BL19) for humanities scholars. The collection comprises over thirty-five thousand unique English-language titles, both fiction and non-fiction, spanning 1700 to 1899. The platform offers comprehensive search functionality by keyword, author, category, and date. A series of short instructional videos on the use and functionality of Curatr is available on the VICTEUR project website.

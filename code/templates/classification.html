Classification Index

From 1823 to the 1980s, the British Library used a topical index of pressmarks indicating the physical arrangement of books on its shelves. This classification index is organised across several levels of granularity, from broad top-level categories (e.g. "Fiction", "Geography", "Medicine") down to more specific topics (e.g. "Geography" → "Ireland and Scotland"). Using a combination of OCR text recognition and manual annotation, we have digitised the higher levels of the index. Browse volumes within these categories by clicking the links below.

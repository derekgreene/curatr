Semantic Network Visualisation

To explore relationships between words and concepts in the British Library Nineteenth Century Digitised Books Collection (BL19), this tool creates and visualises semantic networks. Each node (circle) represents a word or concept, and each edge (link) between two nodes indicates a relationship. To create a network, enter one or more seed words below and click ‘Visualise’. Seed words appear as yellow nodes, and associated words as blue nodes. Double-click a node to view the relevant search results from the BL19 collection. Use the navigation controls, or scroll, to pan and zoom.
